The Best Movies according to me

My top three favourite movies of all time.

  • Rakn 1: The Imitation Game

    "The Imitation Game", focusing on Alan Turing and his groundbreaking work in computer science. His contributions to early computing resonate with my love for mathematics and technology. This is a compelling historical drama that delves into the life of mathematician Alan Turing and his pivotal role in breaking the Enigma code during World War II. The film, directed by Morten Tyldum, beautifully portrays Turing's genius and the groundbreaking work that led to the development of the early computer. Benedict Cumberbatch delivers a powerful performance as Turing, capturing both his brilliance and personal struggles. "The Imitation Game" is a deeply moving film that celebrates the impact of mathematical innovation and the complexities of Turing's life.

  • Rank 2: Gravity

    "Gravity" is an exhilarating and visually captivating film that takes you on a breathtaking journey through space. The movie, directed by Alfonso Cuarón, follows astronauts stranded in orbit after a disastrous accident. The film’s stunning special effects and intense atmosphere create a gripping experience. Sandra Bullock’s performance is both powerful and emotional, capturing the struggle for survival in the vastness of space. "Gravity" is a remarkable cinematic achievement that highlights the resilience of the human spirit.

  • Rank 3: The Martian

    "The Martian" is an inspiring and entertaining sci-fi adventure directed by Ridley Scott. Based on Andy Weir’s novel, it tells the story of Mark Watney, an astronaut stranded on Mars who must use his ingenuity to survive. The film balances humor, science, and a sense of optimism, driven by Matt Damon's engaging performance. "The Martian" offers a realistic and hopeful portrayal of space exploration and survival, making it a memorable and thought-provoking watch.
